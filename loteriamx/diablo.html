EL DIABLO

'El diablo y yo nos entendemos como dos viejos amigos.'

the devil has come and gone for a very long time, sometimes he finds himself in you and you wont even notice. A shadow lurking, a whispered crime, In the quiet corners where intentions may erode us. He wears a smile like a mask in the night, Crafting temptation with a flick of his hand. In moments of weakness, he dances in sight, A puppeteer pulling the strings of your stand. With honeyed words, he sweetens the deal, Promises wrapped in a velvet embrace. Enticing your heart with a false sense of zeal, Leading you down to a perilous place.




Milan Castañeda Porchas
