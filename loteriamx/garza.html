LOTERÍA MEXICANA

LA GARZA

'Como el halcón tras de la garza huida, por los espacios seguiré tu vuelo'

wings of love propel my ascent; if you go to heaven, I will arrest you in heaven... THE VISION. It is the biggest drop. THE POET. Know who you are, virgin of flatterers eyes, which the dew veiled from me before; Slight Cendal reveals your little ones round breasts, to my attempt... THE VISION. The fairy of dreams. THE POET.Ah! I look at you in the far expanse much more beautiful the more naked...- Are you fleeing the human sensation?" Does your heart perhaps fear doubt?... THE VISION. Tomorrow's tedium. I am the heron that the hawk holds, Seeing the farthest horizons: when your restless ambition overtakes me, Remember! Will break in your hands the poet's lyre.






Milan Castañeda Porchas
