LOTERÍA MEXICANA

EL VIOLONCELLO

'había un violoncello entre el café y el humo de pitillos y cien aulas con libros amarillos'

In shadows deep, the cello sighs, Its curves embrace the night’s soft cries.Strings whisper tales of love and woe, In every note, a heartbeat’s glow. Resonance flows like rivers wide, A soulful dance, where dreams reside. With bow in hand, the world stands still, As music blooms, the heart does fill.Each stroke a journey, a tale untold, Of passion fierce, and moments bold. Melodies weave through the twilight air, A tapestry rich, woven with care. In the hush of dusk, the echoes play, Inviting the stars to join the sway. With every crescendo, the spirit soars, Unlocking the soul, opening doors.






Milan Castañeda Porchas
